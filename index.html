NAMUNDERA JUNIOR SCHOOL

ORIGIN

Namundera junior school was began in the year 2023 and domiciled in Namundera primary school with Mr. Waluanda John as the head of institution. The government of Kenya latter posted four teachers to the school. The teachers were Benard Wechuli, Beatrice Cherono, Kevin Wanyama and Shamim Kasawa. The named teachers implemented the Competency Based Curriculum incoporating the new pedagogy as adviced during retooling.

LOCATION

Namundera Junior School is located in western region, Kakamega county, Navakholo sub county, Bunyala west ward
